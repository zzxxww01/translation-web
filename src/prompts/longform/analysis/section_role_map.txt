## Task
For every section, explain:
- what job it does in the article,
- how it connects to the previous section,
- how it sets up the next section,
- which points a translator must not weaken,
- and which translation risks are specific to that section.

Additionally, for each section, analyze the paragraph-level structure:
- **Paragraph roles**: Identify the role of each paragraph (引入论点/展开论证/举例说明/对比分析/总结收束/过渡段落)
- **Key transitions**: Mark important transition words or phrases (However/Therefore/Moreover/In contrast/As a result)
- **Paragraph relations**: Describe how paragraphs connect (并列/递进/转折/因果/总分)

This paragraph-level analysis helps translators maintain logical flow and choose appropriate Chinese connectors.

## Paragraph Role Definitions
- **引入论点**: Introduces a new argument or claim
- **展开论证**: Develops and elaborates on an argument
- **举例说明**: Provides examples or evidence
- **对比分析**: Compares or contrasts different approaches/ideas
- **总结收束**: Summarizes or concludes a section
- **过渡段落**: Transitions between topics or sections

## Paragraph Relation Types
- **并列**: Parallel points at the same level
- **递进**: Progressive development (building on previous point)
- **转折**: Contrast or opposition
- **因果**: Cause-and-effect relationship
- **总分**: Summary-to-detail or detail-to-summary

## Rules
- Cover every section id that appears in the summaries.
- Keep `key_points` focused on the section's argument payload, not generic topic labels.
- Keep `translation_notes` operational: terminology, tone preservation, annotation strategy, or likely translationese risks.
- Do not wrap the JSON in Markdown.
